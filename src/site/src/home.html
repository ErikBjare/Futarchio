What is Futarchio?

Futarchio [Fute - r - kio] is a platform where you can vote for your values and make predictions based on your beliefs. This effectively implements the two major components in a system of government called a Futocracy, where you "Vote on values, but bet beliefs". This combo has many potential uses. Prediction markets and similar platforms can be used to find out who can make the most accurate predictions, or simply be used as a tool to test your own predictions against future evidence.

Rewarding accurate predictions

In a normal prediction market, you bet money on the outcome. We are not a normal prediction market however, here we only deal with reputation and other data being indicative of a users performance. The reason for doing this is that when you are financially vested in the outcome of a prediction, you might be able to use money to manipulate the outcome and make profit. We might be wrong about the risks associated, but at this point in time we'd rather not take the risk nor do we have the resources to implement it (yet).

Usecases

Improving Democracy

An uninformed public is one of the most dangerous things in a democracy. We believe our platform to be a useful tool for every democracy in the world by doing the following:
  1. Let people vote on their values instead of politicians.
  2. Let people predict the outcomes of reforms and policies according to their beliefs.
  3. Let the votes and predictions be indicators for which reforms and policies to implement.

Validity of studies
