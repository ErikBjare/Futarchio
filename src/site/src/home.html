What is Futarchio?

Futarchio [Fute - r - kio] is a platform where you can vote for your values and make predictions based on your beliefs. This effectively implements the two major components in a system of government called a Futocracy, where you "Vote on values, but bet beliefs". We believe this can be a useful tool for every democracy in the world by doing the following:

  1. Let people vote on their values instead of politicians.
  2. Let people predict the outcomes of reforms and policies according to their beliefs.
  3. Let the votes and predictions be indicators for which reforms and policies to implement.
